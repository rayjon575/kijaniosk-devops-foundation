# Region and Availability Zones

## Region Selection
- Chosen region: **closest to majority of users** (e.g., US East)
- Low latency for customers and fast response times
- Compliance with data residency regulations

## Multi-AZ Strategy
- Deploy services across **multiple Availability Zones**
- Ensures high availability in case one AZ fails
- Provides automatic failover and minimal downtime
- Database replicas and application instances distributed for reliability
